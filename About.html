About Us

Our Story

Little Angels Home NPO started with a simple yet powerful goal: to make a difference in the lives of children who need it most. It all began when our founder, Nape Makgopa, saw firsthand the challenges faced by children in Braamfontein. Motivated to create a safe and caring space for them, Nape Makgopa founded Little Angels Home NPO, with the dream of giving these kids the love, care, and support they deserve. Over time, we’ve grown into a vital part of the community, helping countless children and becoming a source of hope for many.

Mission & Vision

Our mission at Little Angels Home NPO is to uplift and empower children who are facing tough situations. We work hard to break the cycle of poverty by providing essential resources like education, healthcare, and emotional support. Our vision is to create a world where every child, no matter their background, has access to the opportunities they need to succeed. We are dedicated to building a community where kids can grow up healthy, educated, and confident, ready to make a positive impact on the world.
